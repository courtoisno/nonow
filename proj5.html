The Hot list

Book, research

2015

The Hot List is an investigation through porn in advertisment strategies and aim to explore the thin gap between those two fields. As in advertisement, pornography is perfect to sale, it's a fact. But where is the limit of it? When should it be censured? Why did we became insensible to them? Pornography has infiltrated the mainstream culture - or say - the representation of pornography. In advertisement, sex is shown to the viewer but yet this access to sex is denied, triggering then his desire. Therefore, advertisers are hoping that the consumer will crave for the product in order to complete the circle of desire.
