Babble font

Typeface

2016

(noun) A baffling stream of words, to utter sounds or words imperfectly, indistinctly, or without meaning.

This typeface is a solution for 'babble' and help visualises the sound accented letters define.
In my native language (French), accented letters are a big part of our everyday vocabulary and
I got aware on how a mispronunciation can change an entire word. This typeface is an experimental attempt on deducing how the words should be pronounced and on an other hand, it gives a bit more 'color' into the words, the same way accented letters do in speaking language. Each accented letter is designed individually regarding the sound they produce ; rising sound, low sound, etc.
