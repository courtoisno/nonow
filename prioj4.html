Ed&Cie is honoring self publishing by organizing independent book fairs. Ed&Cie came up to life by a commun wish to give visibility to smaller publisher, artists, students - and proposing a different kind of book fairs in the Brussels scene. It is also a moving library, travelling from Galleries to various shops. Everyone who share an object linked to publishing is welcome to participate : students, self publishers, studios, collectives, artists and so on. It is an heterogeneous mix of personalities, conceptions, wishes, opinions, supports and styles where plays together all kinds of people.
