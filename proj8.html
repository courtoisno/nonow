With as many colors

Identity

2015

"I am specically interested in covering here, not conventional, but instead what is known as forbidden archaeology. That which challenge the conventional view and the comfort zone we tend to call REALITY"

This project is a screen exhibition and an attempt on revealing the tresures and artefacts found in the shallows of the internet of old. Those I call 'digital paintings' or 'digital posters' were evrywhere on our old webpage under the form of tiny icons. This exhibition tend to reveal their original colors, shapes & design in a poster exhibition perspective and by doing so, celebrating the network when everything began.
