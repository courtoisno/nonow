Nothing but clouds

Art book

2015

→ Collaboration with : Kristina Jurotchkin & Sara Arzu
→ Selected in the 12 Students' submissions @ 'It's a book 2016', Independant bookfair, Liepzig
→ Exhibited in BÜKÜ (Büro für kulturelle Übersetzungen), Liepzig, 20, 21 & 22 Oct 2015

Nothing but Clouds showcases pictures of found objects and landscapes which recall sceneries from a science fiction world or a dystopia. The title of the book is the quotation borrowed from the film 'Solaris' (1972) by Andreij Tarkowski : Nothing but Clouds - is what the scientists can identify on the film that had been meant to be the proof of the existence of the foreign world, the planet 'Solaris'. The story plays with the concepts of the unknown and the unconscious, it is a hint to an immaterial world of our thoughts and imagination. This book is an attempt on creating a similar feeling.
